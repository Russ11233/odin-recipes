Pizza!

All about the better italian inspired food

Pizza is better than lasagna in every way. It is unequivical. There will be no discussion on this topic.

Ingredients

  • Doughb
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Litterly anything.

Steps

  1. Lay the dough into a circle, square, or other geometric shape of your choice.
  2. Spread a thin layer of suace to cover the surface of the dough leaving about 1" of space on the edges.
  3. Distribute the cheese across the layer of suace.
  4. Layer any other toppings you choise onto this.
  5. Put the pie into the oven at a reasonable temperature for a reasonable amount of time.
  6. Take out of the oven when it is done.
  7. Serve and eat! Or just eat if you are alone.
