Burgers!!!

All about the better German inspired food

Burgers is better than lasagna in every way. It is unequivical. There will be no discussion on this topic.

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Bun
  • Mustard
  • Onions
  • NOTHING ELSE

Steps

  1. Cook the meat, beat the meat, and serve the meat, in that order.
  2. Put the meat on a bun.
  3. Put the onion on the meat.
  4. Put the mustard on the onion.
  5. Put the bun on the mustard.
